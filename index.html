About

Hi! I am a PhD student at the Department of Engineering, University of Cambridge, where I am fortunate to be supervised by Prof. Stephan Goetz and advised by Prof. Per Ola Kristensson. My current research interests include 3D vision, world modelling, and spatial audio.

Prior to my doctoral studies, I completed an MSc in Artificial Intelligence at Imperial College London, where I was grateful to be supervised by Prof. Lorenzo Picinali and Prof. Aidan O. T. Hogg. For my undergraduate education, I received a BEng in Electrical and Electronic Engineering from University College London, where I had the opportunity to work under Prof. Miguel Rodrigues.

If you would like to discuss related ideas or shared topics, please feel free to reach out by email. I am always happy to have insightful conversations and discuss potential collaborations. I also welcome review invitations for conferences and journals in related fields.
